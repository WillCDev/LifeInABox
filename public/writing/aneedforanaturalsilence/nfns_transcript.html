Peace and Quietness
I am quite addicted to
Sounds I want to hear
Of birds in trees singing loud
Not human chat and traffic.

Can’t be out too long
Can’t control the outdoor sounds
Cars beeping, men shout
Confusing, conflicting ears
Brain can’t seem to process it.

My cat’s deep purring
Soothes me gently into sleep
Humans can’t do that.
Don’t mind her meows at all
Beats a human nagging me.

Free from human noise
Hear the wind blow through the trees
The breeze through the grass
Even the cries of the gulls
A calm voice whispers through these.
